My Top 10 Favorite Foods

By Julia VanPutte

  1. Spaghetti

    2. Spaghetti is an authentic Italian food with pasta and tomato sauce.

  2. Whipped Cream

    3. Whipped Cream is basically heaven put into a can.

  3. Doughnuts

    4. Doughnuts are wonderful. They are circular pieces of fired dough coated in glaze.

  4. Queso

    5. Queso is like liquid gold. It is melted mexican cheese, and the best queso is from Moes. Usually, people dip corn chips in it.

  5. Sushi

    6. Sushi is a tradiational Japanese dish with raw fish, rice, and vegetables.

  6. Bagels

    7. Bagels are boiled and baked bread rings originating in Poland.

  7. Mac and Cheese

    8. Mac and Cheese is pasta coated in cheese. It is originally an Italian dish. It best when it is homemade.

  8. Ice Cream

    9. Ice Cream is a frozen dessert. The have many flavors, but my favorite it mint chocolate chip.

  9. Pizza

    10. Pizza is an Italian bread dish topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings.

  10. Fried Rice

    11. Fried Rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.
